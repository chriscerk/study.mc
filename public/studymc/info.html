StudyMC

Info


StudyMC (Study Medicinal Chemistry) is a College of Pharmacy web application to help students in studying their course materials. The three main modules within each course topic are explained in detailed below.


  Learn

Content provided to help expand the student's knowledge along with practice problems which include feedback.

  Test

Example test questions to gauge a student's knowledge on a particular topic.

  Review

A review sheet which students fill each section then print the review worksheet upon completion. The "Autocomplete" button on the right fills the worksheet out for you.


How to Save Review Worksheet as a PDF Video

After clicking the "Print!" button, a print page will appear. From the print page, Click on "Change..." under Destination in the top left side of the screen. A window will popup. Under "Local Destinations" click "Save as PDF." The window will close. In the top left side of the screen, click the "Save" button.
